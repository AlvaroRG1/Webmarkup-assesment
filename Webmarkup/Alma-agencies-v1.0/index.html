Aruba

Aruba is one of the three ABC islands, along with Curacao and Aruba. The island of Aruba has a dry climate unlike much of the island in the Caribbean. The warm and dry weather gives the island clear and sunny skies the whole year long. This gives Aruba a nice weather for snorkeling, diving, swimming and sightseeing.

Bonaire

Bonaire is one of the three ABC islands, along with Curacao and Aruba. Bonaire became a special municipality whit in the country of the Netherlands in 2010. The island poses an aired climate, that attracts visitor seeking warm and sunny weather all year around. West of the main island of Bonaire across the sea is also Klein Bonaire situated (Little Bonaire), with sandy white beaches and a beautiful coral reef.

Curaçao

Curaçao is one of the three ABC islands, along with Aruba and Bonaire. Curaçao is the biggest of the three island and is also known for its sandy white beaches and a beautiful coral reef. Curaçao poses an aired climate, that attracts visitor seeking warm and sunny weather all year around. The nice weather makes Curaçao one off the best vacation spots for tourist. This is why Curaçao was crowned as one off the Caribbean most beautiful island.
